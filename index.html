Who is Tulip?
Tulip is a US-based holistic and functional healthcare platform. We are on a mission to make holistic healthcare accessible and affordable by helping our patients thrive through nutrition, exercise, mindfulness, sleep hygiene, and many other appropriate lifestyle changes.
We empower patients to be their healthiest versions
We make personalized functional lab and supplement recommendations, without diagnosing or treating.

Our goal is to educate patients on what certain symptoms can indicate, help them understand the importance of listening to their bodies, and know that there are many natural solutions to support their conditions.
Here's where you come in
We want our patients to have the best holistic care possible, so we are creating a community of the finest integrative and natural healthcare practitioners who are leaders in their field.

We will refer our patients to doctors in their state for virtual visits, where they can have more personalized care in the comforts of their own homes. Once a patient is referred to you, they become your patient and continue their wellness journey.
